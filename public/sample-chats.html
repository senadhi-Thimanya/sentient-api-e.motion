Managing Anxiety

🌐 English
💬 Text input
I have a big presentation tomorrow and I can't stop worrying about it.
Pre-event anxiety is very common and shows you care about doing well. 🌟
Let's reframe: What's the worst that could realistically happen? Anxiety inflates risks far beyond reality.
Try the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique tonight before bed to calm your nervous system.
🎙️ Voice input
What if I forget what I want to say?
That fear is normal! Here are some strategies:
Bullet points, not scripts — easier to recall
Practice out loud 2-3 times — not more
Pause when needed — silence feels longer to you than your audience
You're more prepared than you think. 🌟

🌐 English

What you're describing sounds like burnout — recognizing it is an important first step. 🔥
Burnout often involves: Emotional exhaustion, Cynicism toward work, and a reduced sense of accomplishment.
